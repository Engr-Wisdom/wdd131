Cross River, Nigeria

Flag of Cross River State

Cross River State is located in the South-South region of Nigeria, with Calabar as its capital. It shares an international border with Cameroon and is known for its peaceful environment. The state is famous for Obudu Mountain Resort, Tinapa, and Agbokim Waterfalls. It has rich cultural heritage, especially from the Efik, Ejagham, and Bekwarra people. Agriculture, tourism, and commerce drive its economy. Cross River is also known for hosting the colorful and world-famous Calabar Carnival.
